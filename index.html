The Process

Three stages, zero shortcuts

01

Source

We travel to origin during each harvest season. Lots are cupped on-site, and only those scoring above 86 are selected. We pay 40% above fair-trade minimums.

02

Roast

Each lot gets its own profile on our Loring S35. We develop slowly, avoiding baked flavors, pushing toward clarity. Every batch is cupped by three team members before release.

03

Deliver

Beans are packed in compostable, nitrogen-flushed bags within 24 hours of roasting. We ship Tuesday and Friday to ensure peak freshness at your door.

Brew Guide

Getting the best
from your beans

Our light to medium roasts reward patience. Here is how we brew at the roastery.

  1. Weigh and grind

    Use 15g of coffee per 250ml of water. Grind medium-fine, similar to table salt.

  2. Bloom

    Pour 40ml of water at 94C over the grounds. Wait 30 seconds to let CO2 escape.

  3. Pour in stages

    Add the remaining water in slow, concentric circles. Total brew time: 3 to 4 minutes.

  4. Taste and adjust

    If sour, grind finer. If bitter, grind coarser. Let it cool slightly for the sweetness to open.
